ClozeFox Language Test Plug-in for Firefox

You are trying to learn a foreign language or teach one, e.g. English. You would like to have fill in the blanks tests (cloze tests) with authentic and original material (such as newspaper articles, magazine articles, blog entries, etc.). You'd like to keep your scores, details about them and share them if you like. Now you can have these without leaving the comfort of your favorite web browser, Mozilla Firefox. ClozeFox plug in turns your daily browsing experience into a simple social language learning tool.

Installation is very simple: just click on the Install button on the top right corner of the page and the ClozeFox plug in is going to be installed easily.

Usage is simple too. Once you install ClozeFox, visit a web page, e.g. Franz Schubert on Wikipedia (or right click, go to the ClozeFox menu group on your context menu and choos 'Suggest a Page'). Right click with your mouse on the page and in the context menu that appears you'll see the ClozeFox menu group, select it and then select a test strategy, e.g. Preposition Test. You'll immediately see that the page you are looking at is now converted to a language test. Test your knowledge for English prepositions and then once you are done again right click and in the ClozeFox menu, select "Calculate Score". A small notification will pop up at the bottom right corner of your screen and your score will be saved. If you want to see your previous scores and the pages just click on the Slide Bar and click on the ClozeFox icon to see a list.

That's all. For now! ;-)
