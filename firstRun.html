How to Use ClozeFox

You are trying to learn a foreign language or teach one, e.g. English. You would like to have fill in the blanks tests (cloze tests) with authentic and original material (such as newspaper articles, magazine articles, blog entries, etc.). You'd like to keep your scores, details about them and share them if you like. Now you can have these without leaving the comfort of your favorite web browser, Mozilla Firefox. ClozeFox plug in turns your daily browsing experience into a simple social language learning tool.

Usage is very simple. Once you install ClozeFox, visit a web page, e.g. Franz Schubert on Wikipedia (or right click, go to the ClozeFox menu group on your context menu and choose "Suggest a Page"). Right click with your mouse on the page and in the context menu that appears you'll see the ClozeFox menu group, select it and then select a test strategy, e.g. Preposition Test or Random Test:



If ClozeFox can detect the

and language of the page, you'll immediately see that the page you are looking at is now converted to a language test (otherwise it is notify you of its failure and suggest that you try another page). Test your knowledge for English prepositions or just do a random test, and then once you are done again right click and in the ClozeFox menu, select "Calculate Score". The system will automatically try to post your results to your Twitter account and thus it is going to ask you your Twitter username and password:



However you don't have to do this if you don't want it or if you don't have a Twitter account yet. In that case simply click on the Cancel button and no information is going to be posted. But if you choose to post then you, your friends and maybe your teachers can share / see latest results:



After your score is calculated, on the main page your correct answers are marked with blue whereas your wrong answers are marked with red:



If you want to see your previous scores and get some statistics about them all you have to do is to open the slide bar by clickin on the blue right arrow on the upper left corner of the page:



What if you want to share this test with your friends or your students? ClozeFox lets you copy the current test configuration to your clipboard with a single menu item click. Once you have it copied to your clipboard you can paste it to your e-mail message or post to a forum and share it with anybody in the world:



A notification in the lower right corner indicates that the current web page along with its test configuration is autmatically copied to your clipboard:



That means when your students / friends visit the web address (URL) you sent, they will see the test immediately without needing to right click and select some tests:



If you want to delete your previous scores you can choose "Delete Score Details" from the menu. The system will warn you about this action because there's no UNDO operation for this. If you think deleting previous scores is a bad idea simply click on the Cancel and nothing will be deleted:



If you need to learn the definiton of a word or phrase during your test you can simply select it with your mouse and then click on the yellow define button at the lower right corner of your corner:



Whenever you need help again you can simply select the "Help" menu item from the context menu of ClozeFox.

That's all. For now! ;-)
